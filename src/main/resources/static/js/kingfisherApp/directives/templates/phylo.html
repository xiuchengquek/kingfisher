Edit Clustering Information

The phylogram on the left shows the hierarchical clustering of your mutations. By default, the number of clusters selected is equivalent to the number mutations.

Variant allele fractions that trend together over time indicate likely membership within the same clone; Mutations in the same clusters will appear sequentially after one another and share the same VAF score which is the mean of the cluster For more information on how clustering will affect the fish plot, please click here

To do your clustering, please group your mutations by clicking on the nodes assigning a color using the color palette below.
