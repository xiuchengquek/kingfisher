Introduction

OncoBlocks KingFisher

OncoBlocks is a new open-source initiative, currently hosted at the Biostatistics and Computational Biology Department of the Dana-Farber Cancer Institute. The goal of the project is to create reusable, open source software components to support cancer genomics research and enable precision (or "personalized") cancer medicine.

Project KingFisher a componenet of Oncoblock and is web-based prototype for visualizing and analyzing multiple genomic snapshots over time and space.
